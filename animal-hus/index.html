Our Divisions

We are one of the foremost operators in the Nigerian livestock industry providing livestock feeds, training, premixes, health products, equipment and housing consultation and technical laboratory services for disease diagnosis, feed analysis and research.

Our Operations

Our farm operations began in 1983 as a small scale poultry farm and has grown today to become on of the largest layer farms in Nigeria rearing uniform Point of Lay pullets for farmers accross the country and producing premium table eggs for consumers all over Nigeria.

Our Leadership

Our Leadership consists of veterinarians, poultry scientists, pharmacist, engineers, accountants and animal scientists among others that have over 600 combined years of experience in then livestock industry.
